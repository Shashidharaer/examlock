      ## Login to Your Account

      Learn how to access your ExamLock account and manage your authentication settings.
      ![mushroom](https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2025/09/30/14/53/mushroom-9864616_1280.jpg)

      ### Standard Login
      1. Navigate to the login page at `/login`
      2. Enter your registered email address
      3. Enter your password
      4. Click "Sign In"

      ### SSO (Single Sign-On)
      - Connect with your institution's SSO provider
      - Supported providers: Google Workspace, Microsoft Azure AD, Okta

      ### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
      - Enable MFA for enhanced security
      - Use authenticator apps or SMS verification

      ### Troubleshooting
      - Forgot password? Use the password reset link
      - Account locked? Contact your administrator
