      ## Create Your Account
      
      Get started with ExamLock by creating your account in just a few simple steps.
      
      ### Registration Steps
      1. Visit the registration page at `/register`
      2. Choose your account type:
         - **Institution Admin** - Manage multiple users and exams
         - **Individual Educator** - Create and manage your own exams
         - **Student** - Join exams with invitation codes
      3. Fill in your details:
         - Full name
         - Email address
         - Strong password (min. 8 characters)
         - Institution/Organization name
      4. Verify your email address
      5. Complete your profile setup
      
      ### Account Types & Features
      - **Free Trial**: 14-day trial with full features
      - **Basic Plan**: Essential features for individual educators
      - **Pro Plan**: Advanced features and analytics
      - **Enterprise**: Custom solutions with dedicated support
      
      ### After Registration
      - Set up your first assessment
      - Invite team members
      - Configure security settings
      - Explore the platform features
